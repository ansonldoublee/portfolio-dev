About me

I'm an end-to-end designer and developer. Here's a breakdown of some of the things I can create.

Branding

I create unique and memorable brand identities, from initial concepts and logos to complete rebranding projects. Yes, I created the animated avatar throughout this site!

Design

Focusing on UX/UI, I design responsive web and mobile with tools such as Figma and Miro. My human-centered approach prioritises research and prototyping for intuitive, mobile-friendly designs.

Development

I can also bring website designs to life with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. From concept to execution.
